Cactus Collector

How to play:

1. Click on the random number box to generate a random number.

2. Look at the different cactus photographs. Choose one and click on it. click the cacti to generate a random number.

3. If you choose the right numbers, that add up to the same number as what is shown in the randomnumber box, a small cactus will show up, and you will have won.
